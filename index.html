Patco Website

Challenge: Outdated website misrepresented the brand, resulting in low engagement and unclear communication of services.

Goals: Redesign the site to reflect Patco’s industry expertise, improve credibility, and drive qualified inquiries.

Strategy: Position Patco as a high-trust, solutions-first partner through clear messaging and intuitive UX.

Execution: Rewrote web content to emphasize benefits over features, integrated trust signals, and streamlined calls to action. Improved site speed and refined layout to support navigation and conversions.

Branding: Applied updated visual styling with modern layouts, performance-focused UI, and service-forward hierarchy.

Deliverables: Full website redesign, optimized assets, performance audit, and CMS training for internal updates.

Results: 40% drop in bounce rate, 2× increase in inquiries, and stronger client confidence in Patco’s capabilities.

Cafe Diario

Challenge: Refresh packaging in a saturated market to reflect the brand’s inclusive mission and stand out on retail shelves.

Goals: Create a visual identity that feels approachable and premium, while making coffee selection easier for everyday customers.

Strategy: Reframe coffee as a daily ritual for everyone, blending origin storytelling with clear, friendly language.

Execution: Developed product naming conventions and messaging around flavor and simplicity. Designed packaging with bold color blocks, minimal typography, and subtle illustrations to reflect blend origin and tasting notes.

Branding: Created a flexible label system that supports shelf visibility and brand recognition without losing charm.

Deliverables: Packaging design, custom illustrations, iconography, and retail mockups.

Results: Increased retail interest, improved shelf presence, and a reported boost in sales within the first quarter.
